a cigarette case for manon
the meeting of a 6 part mold and a 13 part mold
sophie leamon

i was insired by hailey biebers rhode phone case where she designed an everyday object [phone case] around what rhode believes should be equally everyday use. i designed it to make this case perfect for only three cigarettes, the number that manon is hoping to cut down to in her 29th year.

i designed two multi-part molds in fusion360 to cast in silicon and in hydrostone.
