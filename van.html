econoline van build out 2020
plumbing electric and plywood
sophie leamon

designed and construted a covid escape vehicle in early 2020 with rachel kessler, including plumbing, wiring, window-tinting, wood and metal work. the van is equipped with 400 amps of solar paneling, 1,000 watt ad/dc inverter and a 1,200 kW battery that powers both an ac and dc system simultaneously. has two ac outlets, four dc overhead lights, and dc energy effiient refrigerator and an integrated water pump and drain. the interior has a road-safe folding bench seat that fits two and two large back storage pull outs that can each hold up to 500 lbs.
